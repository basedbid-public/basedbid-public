# how it works

---

Pools

Create a bonding curve token launch with a custom market cap target. Free to
deploy. Once the target is hit the liquidity migrates automatically to the
relevant DEX (Raydium on Solana, Uniswap/PancakeSwap on EVM chains).

The key difference: the creator earns 1-10% of all trading volume after
graduation, forever. Not just during the bonding curve. On any DEX the token
trades on.

Set custom graduation marketcap, whitelisting and launch protections.

---

Flash Tokens

Deploy a token directly to a DEX instantly using virtual liquidity. Zero
upfront capital required, no bonding curve, no wait. Trading is live from
the first swap and fees start collecting immediately.

Custom solutions allow fees captured at the swap event or from LP Fees or both. This enables incredibly dynamic, programmatic fee control
raising the limits far beyond existing platforms.

Built for memes, trends, anything that needs to be live in seconds.

---

Boards

Platforms, KOLs, agencies, and community leaders can create a branded whitelabel launchpad under
their own name at based.bid/b/yourname. Projects launch on their Board, the
Board owner earns a percentage of all trading fees from every token on their
board, forever.

No development work required to set one up. Your brand, your community,
your revenue.

---

Fee Builder

Ships Thursday April 10th.

Programmable treasury routing built on top of Pools and Flash Tokens. Instead
of fees going to a single wallet, creators define a split across unlimited
recipients at the point of each swap.

Fees are captured in 100% native assets (ETH, BNB, SOL). The LP is never
touched. No batch sells, no chart impact. The structure can be changed at any
time with no need relaunch.

Example split:
- 3% to creator wallet in ETH
- 1% to marketing wallet in USDC
- 1% to team multisig in USDC
- 0.5% to buyback contract
- 0.5% to holder rewards

BasedBid takes 0.5% on top of whatever the creator sets. Creator always
controls 100% of their own fee allocation.

Set 5% and have 5% routed exactly as your describe. Maximum transparency.

---

Buyback support

When a token launched on based.bid hits the agreed market cap milestone, our platform uses
50% of its own protocol for revenue generated by that token to buy it back from
the open market. Repeats at further milestones. Demonstrated live with
$MIDTERMS at the $50k and $100k marks (so far).

---

Chains

ETH, Base, BNB, Solana, MegaETH. All live.

Tokens graduate to the native DEX on each chain. Fee collection works
post-graduation on any DEX the token trades on.
